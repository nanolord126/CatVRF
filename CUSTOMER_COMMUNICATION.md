## 📊 FAQ RESPONSES

### Q: How does payment work?

```
We handle all payments automatically:

1. Viewer pays (via card, SBP, wallet, crypto)
2. Platform processes transaction
3. Earnings credited to your wallet
4. You can withdraw anytime (min ₽500)

Commission: 14% of transaction

All payments are secure and encrypted.
```

### Q: What are NFT gifts?

```
NFT gifts are digital collectibles your audience sends you:

Each gift level:
- Bronze: Common, fun to collect
- Silver: Rare, higher value
- Gold: Very rare, collector's items
- Platinum: Ultra-rare, exclusive

After 14 days, any gift can be upgraded to next tier!

They're stored on blockchain, so they're truly yours.
```

### Q: How do I verify my account?

```
Verification requires:
1. ID photo (passport, driver's license)
2. Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
3. Selfie with ID

Process:
1. Upload documents → 
2. Our team reviews (usually 24 hours) → 
3. Approved (or we'll ask for clarification)

Once verified, you can receive payments!
```
